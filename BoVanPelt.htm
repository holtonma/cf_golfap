Bo Van Pelt: A rising star on the PGA Tour

FULL NAME: Bo  Van Pelt
HEIGHT: 6-4
WEIGHT: 200
BIRTHDATE: May 16, 1975
BIRTHPLACE: Richmond, IN
RESIDENCE: Tulsa, OK; plays out of GC of Oklahoma (Broken Arrow, OK)
FAMILY: Wife, Carrie; Olivia
EDUCATION: Oklahoma State University (1998, General Business)

JOINED TOUR: 1999

- Finished the year No. 5 on the final Nationwide Tour money list with $289,248
- Made the cut in 19 of 24 events on the 2003 Nationwide Tour with an incredible 16 top-25 finishes.
- Made 13 straight cuts through the Omaha Classic.
- Shot rounds of 64-67-69-62 to win his first career title at the Omaha Classic. ( 26-under-par 262 equaled the lowest 72-hole score in Nationwide Tour history.)
- Top-10 finishes: Miccosukee Championship (T3), Henrico County Open (T4), Nationwide Tour Championship (T5), First Tee Arkansas Classic (T6), Lake Erie Charity Classic at Peek'n Peak Resort (T7) and The Reese's Cup Classic (T10).
- Other Top-15 finishes: Envirocare Utah Classic (T14), Oregon Classic (T11) and the Albertsons Boise Open (T11)
