It is with great pleasure that Albatross has the opportunity to tell you about Todd Demsey's game and character. 

Todd Demsey is an NCAA Division I Individual Champion from Arizona State, who has played the PGA Tour and Nationwide Tour since 1995, including a year on the PGA Tour in 1997.  In 2003, despite undergoing two surgeries to remove a brain tumor, Demsey finished in the top 25 nine times, and completed the season with 17 of 22 cuts made, good enough for 42nd on the money list.  A person with tremendous character and talent, look out for Todd Demsey in 2004 and beyond!  This is a golfer with a history of success, a passion for the game, and a tremendous amount of character.

After qualifying for the US Open sectional, Demsey just missed qualifying in a playoff to make it to the United States Open Championship.
