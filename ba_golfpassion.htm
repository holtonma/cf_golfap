Brad Adamonis, Albatross-Software.com, and Golf Passion
By: Mark A. Holton, CEO and Founder, Albatross-Software.com

There are traits that “don’t show up on the stat sheet”, as the saying goes.  This cliché is omnipresent in all sports and all endeavors due to the truth behind it.  This holds particularly true in the game of golf.

In a telling conversation between BUY.com and PGA Tour player Brad Adamonis and an amateur golfer this year, prior to the amateur’s first round in the US Amateur sectional qualifier, the amateur asked “so, Brad, do you have any tips prior to me teeing it up tomorrow---any last second advice?”  Adamonis’ reply was to the point:

 “This game is played with your heart and your head.  Remember that.”

In addition to his talents as a ball striker (292.4 yard average on the BUY.com tour), chipper and putter Brad Adamonis is a player who has a passion for the game, and a fiery competitive streak that is without parallel.  While he has world class talents on the golf course (see Bio), it is this passion and competitive fire that has brought Adamonis to where he is today, and that which will bring him to loftier competitive heights as he gains experience on major professional golf tours.  It is this passion that has driven him to mold his compact, powerful swing, refine his touch, and learn from each success and failure.

Irrespective any stat, it is this inner drive that inspires. It is in the fires of this type of golf passion that Albatross-Software.com was forged. This desire to play the game at the best of one’s ability is rare, like the double-eagle, or “Albatross”.  Many people claim this unique love for the game and drive to be their best, though few bear the scars of those truly dedicated.

Go the practice tee at a local PGA Tour event this year, a Nationwide Tour event, or even at your local country club.  You will hear the clicks and cracks of irons and woods from players you may or may not have heard of or seen, while watching them hit shots with piercing trajectories on frozen ropes towards their targets.  Why are they lined up inside the ropes on that range, instead of you, or the pro down the street?  Why are this year’s US Amateur finalists, and US PubLinks champion playing in the Masters instead of you, or why did your roommate make the college golf team instead of you, or last year’s high school state champion win, while you didn’t qualify?  Sure there are economic and physical factors that could be offered as reasons, or more accurately, as excuses.  But club, state, regional, and national champions are crowned every year from public courses without access to driving ranges---the simple truth is, they wanted it more than you, they’ve probably wanted it for a longer than you, and they did whatever it took to relentlessly hunt down their goal and bring it home.  Their passion drove them to find a way to win.

In many conversations with Adamonis over the course of his past year on the BUY.com Tour, he’s explained, “All I can do, is prepare myself to play each day to the best of my ability, and control my thoughts and emotions on the golf course.  The rest is out of my hands.”  

It is our firm conviction at Albatross-Software.com that the software tools we have innovated to date, and the tools we continue to improve and develop will consistently improve your preparation for the level of play you wish to reach and maintain.  This is the manifestation of our passion for the game, and the passion and professionalism typified by our pro tour partner, Brad Adamonis.  We are honored and privileged to accept his offer to endorse our product and add his counsel and suggestions.  Brad--all the best in golf.
